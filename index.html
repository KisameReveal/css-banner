My Turn ons and Turn offs when looking for a partner

What are your 3-5 turn ons when looking for a partner?

  1. Kindness: A warm heart and a gentle nature can be incredibly attractive. Someone who is kind to others and compassionate is a major turn-on.
  2. Intelligence: Being able to have deep, meaningful conversations and learn from each other is very appealing. Intellectual compatibility is important.
  3. Adventurous Spirit: Someone who loves to explore, try new things, and is open to new experiences can be very exciting to be around.
  4. Confidence: Not to be confused with arrogance, a person who is confident and comfortable in their own skin is very attractive.
  5. Humility: The ability to be humble, admit mistakes, and show gratitude is a wonderful quality in a partner.

What are your 3-5 turn offs when looking for a partner?

  1. Dishonesty: Trust is fundamental in any relationship. Lying or being deceitful is a significant turn-off.
  2. Selfishness: Constantly putting oneself first and not considering others' needs and feelings can be very unattractive.
  3. Rudeness: Being rude or disrespectful to others, including waitstaff or strangers, is a major turn-off.
  4. Lack of Ambition: Not having any goals or drive in life can be unattractive. It's important to be with someone who has aspirations.
  5. Jealousy: Excessive jealousy and possessiveness can be very off-putting. Trust and independence are important in a healthy relationship.
